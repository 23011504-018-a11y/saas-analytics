# Weekly OKR-Style Goals

## Objective 1: Reduce churn risk
- Objective: Reduce preventable churn in the next 30 days.
- Key Results:
  - Reduce churned customers from 33.3% to below 20% by month end.
  - Re-engage at least 50% of customers with low usage or long inactivity.
  - Lower average support tickets for at-risk accounts by 20%.

## Objective 2: Improve retention quality
- Objective: Strengthen revenue retention and reduce contraction.
- Key Results:
  - Improve NRR from 82.1% toward 100%+.
  - Reduce revenue churn from 24.3% to below 15%.
  - Increase expansion MRR from healthy accounts each month.

## Objective 3: Improve activation and product adoption
- Objective: Help more customers reach product value faster.
- Key Results:
  - Increase average usage score for new customers by 10%.
  - Raise onboarding completion rate for new accounts.
  - Reduce time-to-first-value for new customers.

## Weekly review cadence
- Review progress every Monday.
- Update status as On Track, At Risk, or Off Track.
- Escalate blockers to leadership within 48 hours.
